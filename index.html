About

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Technical University of Denmark where I lead the student-based research organization, Deep Learning Copenhagen, together with Jose Juan Almagro Armenteros (Ph.D. student, BioInformatics). I previously did a one-year internship at Salesforce Research supervised by Richard Socher. I got my M.Sc. honors from the Technical University of Denmark supervised by Ole Winther. I am interested in Natural Language Processing, Generalization, Reinforcement Learning, BioInformatics, and ChemInformatics.
